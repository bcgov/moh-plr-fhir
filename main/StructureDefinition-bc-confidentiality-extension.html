Extension: BC Confidentiality Extension

Official URL: http://hlth.gov.bc.ca/fhir/provider/StructureDefinition/bc-confidentiality-extension Version: 1.0.0
Active as of 2025-05-30 Computable Name: ConfidentialityExtension

Indicates the confidentiality of a BC provider's (practitioner or organization) information.

Context of Use

    This extension may be used on the following element(s)

    • Element ID: Practitioner
    • Element ID: Organization
